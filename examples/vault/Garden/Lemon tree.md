---
title: Lemon tree — repotting plan
tags: [garden]
plant: Meyer lemon
---

Roots circling at the bottom of the current pot. Up-pot to ~14" terracotta, gritty mix, late spring after last frost. Don't over-water for the first two weeks; let it find the new soil.

Cuttings to try: take 4–6" semi-hardwood pieces in early summer, dip in rooting hormone, perlite + peat. Bag over the top. Bottom heat helps.

Pollination indoors: a small paintbrush, midday, every other day during bloom.
