title: Marginalia mode
tags: [ideas, skein]
status: half-formed
---

What if the assistant's replies behaved like marginalia? They'd sit in the gutter, you could pull them in, but they'd default to "in the margin" not "in the page."

The current chat sidebar is fine, but it imposes a strict spatial separation between "what I wrote" and "what the model wrote." Real margins are messier — sometimes you copy a sentence in, sometimes you draw an arrow, sometimes you just keep walking.

Open question: does the marginalia get embedded? Or do they only count once you "pull them in"? I think the latter — a margin note is a half-thought; the page is what you committed to.
