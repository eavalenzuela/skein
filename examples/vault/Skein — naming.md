---
title: Skein — naming
tags: [skein, etymology]
---

A skein is a length of yarn loosely coiled. Also a flock of wild geese in flight. Both feel right. Threads loosely held. Things that travel together without being tied.

Other candidates I'm letting go of:

- **Filum** — too quiet on the tongue.
- **Cairn** — beautiful, but the metaphor is "you stack stones to mark a path I've already walked," and notes aren't only that.
- **Axon** — leans too hard into the AI angle.

Skein keeps the warm-tactile thing without leaning on the AI thing. And the geese.
